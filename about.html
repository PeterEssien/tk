About Us

At Crypto-Home, we see investing differently. Today’s financial system is complex, exclusive and expensive - making it hard for the typical person to start investing. So, we’re building a platform without digital walls and complex barriers. A platform that empowers you to see new possibilities for your money - and helps make them a reality

Who We Are

Crypto-Home was founded in 2014 in Vermont, United States with the aim of making investing accessible for everyone. Our goals since day one was to reimagine what it means to invest, by making simple, easy-to-use financial products for everyone. Now, 8 years later, and with more than 730 team members and 3.5 million users, we're one of the fastest growing fintechs in America, Asia & Europe. With a PSD2 payment service provider licence and E-Money licence, state-of-the-art security and streamlined user experience, we make it possible for both first-time investors and seasoned experts to invest in what they believe in. Our user-friendly, trade-everything platform empowers you to invest in the stocks, cryptocurrencies and metals you want — with any amount of money.

Crypto-Home intended target audience includes those who are interested in digital currencies and more specifically cryptocurrencies. We offer high quality and up-to-date information which can aid users when making decisions within a digital currency ecosystem. For example, a user interested in investing bitcoin can fill the form and an agent would get back to you immediately with the follow up steps.
